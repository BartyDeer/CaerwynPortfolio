About Caerwyn,
an extremely passionate graduate game designer.

I have been passionate about game development for as long as I can recall. The ability to create expansive, thought provoking worlds that utilise uniquely interesting mechanics as a means to capture an audience and provide them with an engaging experience is one that I not only admire, but strive to learn and develop myself.

I've followed game development throughout my life, starting with small scratch and monogame games in school, eventually finding the Academy of Interactive Entertainment where I started to focus my game development efforts into game design and create several small projects that I have put onto my Itch.io page (https://caerwyn.itch.io/ ).

Through those projects I have created several games that I am proud of, they are small and very indie, but creating them was still an amazingly fun experience with many inspiring teammates, expanding my skillset and knowledge of game design and the overall development cycle
