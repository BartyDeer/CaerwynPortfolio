About Liminal Sleep

Liminal Sleep is a first person liminal space1 exploration game originally created over a week in February 2022 for the Brackeys 2022.1 Game Jam where the theme was "It Is Not Real".

1 Liminal Space - 'A location which is a transition between two other locations, or states of being. Typically these are abandoned, and oftentimes empty'

The game fits nicely in the theme "It Is Not Real" through the utilization of non-euclidean level design as a means to emphasise the fact that it is set within the character's own lucid dream.

The non-euclidean level design also coincidentally happens to remove the players anticipated movemennt around the level, and this is hinted at in the dialogue for the game2 ;

2 "You are now trapped and aware in a nightmare you cannot control"

Gameplay.

In Liminal Sleep you have to search a variety of liminal spaces (currently just the hospital & office with more planned in the future) in search of areas of interest marked with red light to open the corresponding red doors.

When going through the starting room and connecting hallway a mannequin follows before disappearing when the player looks away, this leads the player to have a consistant feeling of something following them.
The mannequin also appears inside a small room with flickering lights which serves to confirm for the player the feeling of something following them and have them wondering where it will appear next, with it all coming together at the end when the mannequin follows the player towards a hole at the centre of a red lit room. The jumping down the hole being a comparison to the feeling of when you're jolted out of your sleep, feeling like you were falling.
